About Our Platform

We aim to transform the catering industry by reducing food waste through innovation and technology. Our machine learning-powered solution ensures efficient surplus management and promotes sustainable practices.

Mission

Reducing food waste in catering events through advanced machine learning and smart redistribution systems.

Vision

To build a sustainable, eco-friendly future where no food goes to waste.

Learn More

Our Approach

Our platform leverages the power of machine learning to transform the catering industry by:

  • Predicting Expected diners: Utilizing historical data to forecast Expected diners through which food requirements satisfies and avoid wastage.
  • Suggesting list of Ingredients: Ensuring resources are allocated efficiently for catering events.
  • Surplus Management: Strategically redistributing excess food to minimize environmental impact.
